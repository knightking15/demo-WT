Founder & CEO

LAMINU SHEHU

Founder & Chief Executive Officer

LAMINU SHEHU MD/CEO of White Truth Investment Ltd was born on the 24th of April 1974 in Bauchi, Bauchi State Nigeria. He attended Shehu Garbai primary school maiduguri Federal Government College Maiduguri, Federal University of Technology Yola And Bayero University Kano.

He started his working career in the year 2000 with the Federal Capital Territory Administration as a Planner with the Land Administration Department. He voluntarily retired after ten years of service in 2011 to establish private business. Apart from White Truth Investment Ltd, he founded Khulafawul Rashidin Islamic Academy, Cadlam BDC, Larai Specialist hospital among other businesses.

He has attended several courses within and outside Nigeria, some include leadership course at Northumbria University in UK, Administrative staff college of Nigeria, MCPDP of the Nigerian institute of Town Planner and the Town Planner Registration council. He has visited several countries including Ghana, Togo, Benin, Niger, Ethiopian, Saudi Arabia, United Kingdom, United Arab Emirates among other.Member/Guarantor Nigeria Saudi chamber of commerce, industry and Agricultural and member of Nigerian Environmental Society. He is married with 6 children.
